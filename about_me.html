About Me

Hi, I'm Alexis Trejo, a passionate backend developer with a knack for solving complex problems. Although I don't have a formal degree or professional experience, I've immersed myself in coding and technology through self-learning and hands-on projects.

My journey into backend development began with a fascination for how things work behind the scenes. I started by building small projects and gradually tackled more challenging problems. Each line of code has been a step towards mastering backend development.

Over the past year, I’ve honed my skills in Java, Go, and Python. My projects include creating backend APIs with various architectures and technologies.

Some of my notable personal projects are AT_Drugstore_Backend and Architecture_School_System. Each project has been a valuable learning experience, allowing me to explore new technologies and apply best practices.

Learning is a core part of my journey. I continuously explore new technologies and methodologies to stay updated with industry trends. My goal is to keep growing and adapting in this ever-evolving field.

Computers and technology are not just a profession for me; they are my life and passion. I am deeply engrossed in the world of technology, and coding is more than a job—it's a way of life. Every challenge I encounter, every line of code I write, is a testament to my dedication and love for this field.

Looking ahead, I aim to further develop my skills as a Backend Developer and continue learning every day. I’m excited about the future and the opportunities that lie ahead.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy creating graphic designs on my computer and, outside of the screen, I love reading books and hiking. These interests keep me inspired and energized.

Feel free to explore my projects on GitHub, or connect with me on LinkedIn. I’m always open to new opportunities and collaborations!
